## Overview

Neyyar DMS is a robust, distributed Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system designed specifically for the dairy industry. It bridges the gap between central warehouse management and field-level sales through a unified, data-driven platform, digitizing the supply chain from warehouse to delivery.

## System Architecture

The system is built on a Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) architecture, ensuring that each user has a tailored interface and secure permissions:

1. **Admin**: The central command. Assigns delivery trips, manages global stock matrices, and reviews business analytics.
2. **Accountant**: Handles financial bookkeeping, sales reconciliation, and auditing of stock logs across multiple storehouses.
3. **Salesman**: The field operative. Uses a mobile-optimized PWA to record sales in real-time, update stock on the fly, and follow assigned trip routes.

## Core Modules

### Multi-Warehouse Inventory
Advanced stock management that tracks inventory across multiple physical location. It monitors stock-in and stock-out events and provides real-time visibility into perishable inventory levels to minimize waste.

### Trip and Distribution Engine
A specialized logistics module where Admins create and assign distribution journeys to salesmen. This streamlines the delivery process and ensures every route is accounted for in the global system.

### Business Intelligence
Transforms raw sales data into actionable business analytics. Field operatives update their daily tallies directly from the app, which instantly syncs with the central database for real-time performance tracking and forecasting.

## Outcome

Neyyar DMS takes the complex process of dairy distribution and converts it into a streamlined digital pipeline. By implementing PWA capabilities, salesmen have a native-like experience with offline access, while Supabase provides the real-time syncing and security required for enterprise operations.
