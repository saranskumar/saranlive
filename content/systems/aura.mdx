## Overview

AURA is the flagship event of IEEE WIE SCT SB, requiring a digital platform that reflects its premium standard while providing clear information across multiple technical tracks. The primary goal was to build an immersive experience capable of scaling for high traffic during the registration window.

## System Architecture

The platform was designed with a focus on immersive engagement:

1. **Visual Engine**: A custom "Aura" design system using advanced CSS glassmorphism, glowing gradients, and a deep-space theme.
2. **Modular Navigation**: A track-based layout allowing participants to explore Ideathons, technical bootcamps, and professional development programs independently.
3. **Motion Orchestration**: Scroll-linked animations and viewport-triggered entrance effects to maintain user interest throughout the 6-day program syllabus.

## Implementation

The system featured an interactive timeline component that adapted to mobile users, essential for on-site navigation during the event. Significant effort was placed on optimizing image delivery and asset loading to ensure that high-fidelity visuals did not compromise performance on campus networks. Strategically placed registration flows were integrated with external ticketing platforms to minimize drop-off rates.

## Outcome

The site served as the primary information hub for AURA 4.0, handling all queries regarding schedule, venue, and track-specific details. Its premium aesthetic established a new benchmark for flagship event websites within the IEEE SCT SB ecosystem, maintaining 100% uptime through Vercel deployment during peak registration bursts.

## Lessons Learned

- Micro-interactions, such as hover-glow effects on track cards, significantly improved user time-on-site and engagement.
- Managing a complex 6-day schedule taught valuable lessons in simplifying nested information hierarchy for mobile-first users.
